Service.

Enable our service in your city to help dyslexics in travel. All.

An average of 8% of the population will explore the city in a more comfortable way See EDA. The adopting city can post messages to inform users about travelling services or special events. Not the application nor the city4dys platform will be used to promote private products through advertisements.

Very few spatially enabled application exists for dyslexics: at the time of writing there is only another application: "OpenStreetMap for the dyslexic" made by the same people presenting here this service.
